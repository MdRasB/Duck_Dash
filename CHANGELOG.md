# Changelog

## [1.0.0] - 2026-04-13
### Added
- Full 2D side-scrolling gameplay loop at 60FPS
- Player duck with jump and crouch mechanics
- Obstacle types: Chairs, Bottles, Trees
- Enemy types: Cats, Eagles, Boy, Dog
- Collectibles: Bread, Worm, Cockroach
- Health and Sleep HUD bars 
- Story Mode, Endless Mode, Level Selection, Settings, Pause screens
- Three levels in both story mode, and endless mode
- Custom AssetLoader with intelligent caching and preloading
